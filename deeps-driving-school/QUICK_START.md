1. Go to https://www.emailjs.com/
2. Sign up for free account
3. Follow instructions in `EMAIL_SETUP.md`
4. Test contact form

### Step 2: Set Up Google My Business (15 minutes)
1. Go to https://business.google.com/
2. Add your business
3. Verify with postcard (takes 1-2 weeks)
4. Add photos and hours
