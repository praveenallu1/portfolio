## To Rank for "VicRoads Melbourne" Searches

### 1. Google My Business (CRITICAL)
This is the #1 way to appear in local search results:

1. Go to https://business.google.com/
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Click "Manage your business"
4. Enter: "Deep's Driving School"
5. Add location: South East Melbourne, VIC
6. Add phone: +61 430 191 324
7. Add website: https://www.deepsdrivingschool.com.au
8. Add business hours
9. Add photos of your vehicles and team
10. Verify your business (Google will send a postcard)

**Why this matters**: Google My Business listings appear at the top of search results for local searches like "driving school near me" or "VicRoads lessons Melbourne"

### 2. Local Keywords
The website already targets these keywords:
- "driving school Melbourne"
- "VicRoads test preparation"
- "learner permit training"
- "driving lessons Melbourne"

### 3. Get Backlinks
Search engines rank sites higher when other websites link to you:

- **Local directories**: Add your business to:
  - Yellow Pages Australia
  - Local.com.au
  - Hotfrog
  - Yelp Australia
  
- **Partner websites**: Contact:
  - Local driving schools associations
  - Student forums
  - Melbourne tourism sites

- **Social media**: Link from your Facebook page to website

### 4. Content Strategy
Add blog posts about:
- "How to Pass VicRoads Driving Test in Melbourne"
- "Complete Guide to Victorian Learner Permit"
- "120 Hours Logbook Requirements Explained"
- "Best Driving Routes for Test Practice in Melbourne"
- "VicRoads Hazard Perception Test Tips"

Each blog post should:
- Include target keywords naturally
- Be 800+ words
- Have internal links to other pages
- Include images with alt text

### 5. Reviews & Ratings
- Ask satisfied students to leave Google reviews
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- Aim for 4.5+ star rating
- More reviews = higher ranking

### 6. Local Citations
Ensure your business info is consistent across:
- Google My Business
- Facebook
- Yellow Pages
- Local directories

**Consistency matters**: Same name, phone, address format everywhere
